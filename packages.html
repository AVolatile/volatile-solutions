How Engagement Works

A predictable, milestone-driven delivery process.

1. Discovery

We align on requirements, privacy expectations, and project boundaries.

2. Scope & Estimate

Estimates are non-binding. We supply a fixed-price written proposal for formal approval.

3. Build & Review

Iterative development with structured approval gates and staging access.

4. Launch & Support

Seamless deployment, team training, and a standard 14-day QA warranty.

Privacy-First Architecture

Forms built for minimal data collection. Secure, encrypted handoff of credentials without relying on raw email storage.

Accessibility Aware

We build with WCAG 2.1 AA best practices in mind, ensuring semantic HTML, keyboard focus states, and scalable contrast.

Performance Targets

Optimized DOM footprints, modern image formats, and minimal external libraries to lock in fast Core Web Vitals.
